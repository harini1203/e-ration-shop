About E-Ration Shop

Mission

The goal of E-Ration shop is to save people's time and to reduce corruption.It serves as an interface between the customer and shop handler.The goal of the Public Distribution System in Tamil Nadu is to ensure food security to all citizens, particularly poor people, by making available essential commodities of good quality at affordable prices every month, through fair price shops which are easily accessible.The goal of the Public Distribution System in Tamil Nadu is to ensure food security to all citizens, particularly poor people, by making available essential commodities of good quality at affordable prices every month, through fair price shops which are easily accessible.

1. Objective


  • Elimination of chronic hunger and starvation in Tamil Nadu.

  • Protecting citizens from ill effects of rise in price of essential commodities.

  • Users are made to choose the products available to them based on their needs.

  • Ensuring availability of essential commodities at the right time every month.

  • Ensuring easy accessibility of Fair Price Shops for cardholders via online.

2.Structure of PDS
